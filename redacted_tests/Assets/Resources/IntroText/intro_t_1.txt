On the right you will see the inaccuracies for redaction in the submitted text.

Anything the party wishes to be erased will appear here.

You are to find these inaccuracies within the provided text documents and redact them. 

You perform this redaction by clicking on the word. This will make it disappear. 

So if "biblical names" were to appear in the box you would redact all occurences of these from the provided text documents.

If "losses" were to appear words like "fallen", "lost", "destroyed" would be redacted.
